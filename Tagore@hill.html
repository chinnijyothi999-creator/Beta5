Tagore Hills

Transportation

  • By Air: Birsa Munda Airport (IXR) is the nearest airport, about 12 km from Tagore Hills.
  • By Rail: Ranchi Railway Station is the nearest major railway station, about 7 km from Tagore Hills.
  • By Road: Tagore Hills is well-connected by road, and taxis, auto-rickshaws, and buses are available from Ranchi city.
