Cultural Activities

  • Sarhul Festival: A significant festival celebrated by the tribal communities, marking the beginning of spring and new life. It's a time for nature worship, traditional dances, and community bonding.
  • Traditional Dances: Paika, Chhau, Jhumar, and Tribal Mandar Dance.
  • Nature Worship: Rituals like offering prayers to the Sal tree.
  • Community Feasts: Traditional delicacies like mahua laddoo, dubki, and bamboo shoot curry.
