Transportation

By Air
  • The nearest airport is Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi, approximately 90-100 km away from Hazaribagh.
  • From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Hazaribagh.
By Train
  • The closest major railway station is Hazaribagh Town or Hazaribagh Road Railway Station.
  • Well-connected to major cities like Ranchi, Koderma, and Barkakana.
By Road
  • Hazaribagh is well-connected by road to nearby cities and towns.
  • Regular bus services are available from major cities like Ranchi, Patna, Dhanbad, and Kolkata.
  • National Highway 33 passes through Hazaribagh, making it easily accessible by car or bike.
Local Transportation
  • Auto-rickshaws, cycle-rickshaws, or taxis are available to reach the rock art site.
  • Local buses and taxis are readily available for short distances within the city.

Cultural Activities

Sohrai Art
  • A traditional indigenous art form practiced by women of various ethnic groups.
  • Integral to the Sohrai harvest festival.
Khovar Art
  • A form of wall mural linked to marriages.
  • Practiced by the same women who create Sohrai art.
Rock Art
  • Ancient rock art estimated to be thousands of years old.
  • Showcasing geometric patterns, animal figures, and human life depictions.

Festivals and Celebrations

Sohrai Festival
  • A vibrant celebration of tribal collective spirit.
  • Showcasing gratitude and affection for livestock.
Traditional Dance
  • Features traditional dance performances.
  • Highlighting the region's rich cultural heritage.
