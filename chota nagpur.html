Cultural Activities

  • Tribal Crafts: The tribal communities of Chota Nagpur Plateau are skilled craftsmen, known for their beautiful handicrafts and wood carvings.
  • Traditional Attire: The tribal communities have a distinct traditional attire, which reflects their rich cultural heritage.
  • Language:The region is home to several languages, including Santhali, Mundari, Ho, and Kurux.
