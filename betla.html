Betla Fort

Transportation Options

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Ranchi Airport, which is about 160 km from Betla National Park. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or book a bus to reach Betla.
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is at Latehar, which is about 20 km from Betla National Park. You can take a train to Latehar and then hire a taxi to reach Betla.
  • By Road: Betla National Park is well-connected by road. You can drive to Betla from Ranchi or other nearby cities. Bus services are also available.

Cultural Activities

  • Water Sports: Enjoy boating, kayaking, and jet skiing at lakes like Dimna Lake, Khandoli Lake, and Maithan Lake.
  • Rock Climbing: Challenge yourself at Khandoli Lake, a popular spot for adventure enthusiasts.
  • Nature Walks: Explore the scenic surroundings of lakes, forests, and hills, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.
  • Birdwatching: Spot migratory birds at lakes like Jubilee Lake and Dimna Lake.
